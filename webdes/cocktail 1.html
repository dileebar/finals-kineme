Velvet Sunrise

A magical blend of lavender, blueberry, and sparkling soda. This enchanting cocktail captures the serene beauty of a moonlit night.

Ingredients

  • 1 oz lavender syrup
  • 2 oz blueberry juice
  • 1 oz vodka (optional for a non-alcoholic version)
  • 3 oz sparkling soda
  • Ice cubes
  • Edible glitter (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedge (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Fill a shaker with ice cubes and add lavender syrup, blueberry juice, and vodka (if using).
  2. Shake well until chilled.
  3. Strain the mixture into a tall glass filled with ice.
  4. Top with sparkling soda and stir gently.
  5. Sprinkle a pinch of edible glitter on top for a shimmering effect.
  6. Garnish with a lemon wedge on the rim.
  7. Enjoy your magical Moonlit Dreams cocktail!
